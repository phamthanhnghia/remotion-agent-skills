# Route: remotion-motion-graphics

- **Input:** A brief, a logo, a stat, a quote, or a short concept for a motion unit.
- **Output:** A short (under 10 s), unnarrated, motion-first Remotion composition. Motion is the message.
- **Triggers:** "make a logo sting", "animated stat", "motion graphic for", "short loop", "title card", "lower third", "badge reveal".

## Contract

- Requires: `message` (what the motion communicates), `aspect`, `length` (under 10 s)
- Optional: `loop` (whether it loops), `style_preset`, `brand_colors`

## Interview

Minimal — this is a quick deliverable. Ask only:

1. **What should the motion communicate?** (e.g. logo, stat, quote, badge — must-have if not given)
2. **Aspect ratio?** (16:9 / 9:16 / 1:1 — recommend square)
3. **Should it loop?** (yes / no — recommend no)
4. Run-shape question (b) only: automation or companion?

Skip the storyboard question for motion graphics under 5 s.
