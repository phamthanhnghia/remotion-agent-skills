# Remotion Social Workflow

Author high-energy, vertical 9:16 video clips optimized for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

## Contract & Prerequisites

- **Input:** Short hook statement, key points, social audio, or visual assets.
- **Output:** 1080x1920 (9:16) vertical Remotion composition with fast pacing, bold typography, and visual hooks.
- **Triggers:** "make a TikTok video", "create an Instagram Reel", "YouTube Shorts clip", "9:16 vertical video".

## Design Guardrails

- Aspect ratio: 1080x1920 (9:16).
- Keep text and critical visuals within the mobile safe zone (avoid top bar and bottom UI overlay).
- Fast entrance transitions within the first 2 seconds.
