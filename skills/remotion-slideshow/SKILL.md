# Remotion Slideshow Workflow

Author presentation decks, video pitch decks, and photo/text slide shows using Remotion.

## Contract & Prerequisites

- **Input:** Slide deck structure, markdown outline, images, or presentation brief.
- **Output:** Slide-by-slide Remotion composition with smooth slide transitions (fade, wipe, push, zoom), 30–300 s.
- **Triggers:** "make a slide presentation", "convert pitch deck to video", "slideshow video".

## Key Components

- Use `<Series>` or `<Sequence>` for slide scheduling.
- Implement slide transition effects between `<Series.Sequence>` scenes.
- Support background visual elements, typography hierarchy, and optional narration.
