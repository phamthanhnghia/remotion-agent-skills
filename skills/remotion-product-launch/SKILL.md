# Remotion Product Launch Workflow

Author product showcase and promo videos for SaaS apps, mobile apps, websites, or tech hardware using Remotion.

## Contract & Prerequisites

- **Input:** Website URL, product description, app UI screenshots, or promo brief.
- **Output:** Sleek, high-energy product launch video with UI frame mocks, feature highlights, and call-to-action, 15–90 s.
- **Triggers:** "launch video for X", "product demo video", "showcase our app", "promo for SaaS".

## Core Scene Pipeline

1. **Hero Hook (0–5s):** Dynamic brand reveal & headline proposition.
2. **Feature Cards (5–30s):** Staggered UI device frames, animated feature highlights.
3. **Social Proof / Metrics (30–45s):** Key stats, user quotes, enterprise trust badges.
4. **Call to Action (45–60s):** Product URL & download/signup CTA.
